### Project Goals
PowayServe aims to make community service easier by consolidating local opportunities into one clear, accessible page. Instead of scattered links and confusing sign-ups, residents can quickly find ways to help, register for events, and donate—while the platform tracks impact through simple data-driven metrics (hours served, projects completed, and progress goals).
