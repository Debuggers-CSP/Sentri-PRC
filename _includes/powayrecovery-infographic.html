About

The Poway Recovery Hub is designed to make support pathways easier to find, understand, and trust. Many families and individuals need clear direction during difficult moments. This platform brings local services, meetings, and ways to help into one calm, accessible space.

The system also demonstrates AP CSP concepts with structured resource data, meeting search/filter logic, and conditional recommendation flows based on real user needs.

Impact
Improves awareness of recovery programs, strengthens neighborhood support networks, and connects people and families to the care they need to begin and sustain long-term recovery.
